Twinings Tea Redesign

Packaging & Illustration

Twinings tea is a 319-year-ol tea company originating from London, England—first founded by Thomas Twining in 1706.

To challenge my passion for packaging and illustration, I tried to redesign a popular and traditional tea brand and give it my own twist while staying true to its history. Each box of tea bags is an existing Superblend that provides various benefits, specifically calming, soothing herbal, and probiotic benefits in this case.
