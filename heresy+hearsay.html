Heresy & Hearsay Magazine

Art Direction

Heresy & Hearsay is a playful combination of medieval illuminated manuscripts and modern tabloid culture. This conceptual magazine is a satirical exploration of society’s timeless fascination with status, scandal, and storytelling. It reimagines celebrity gossip through a medieval lens..

This project was a chance to explore art direction and visual storytelling, allowing me to experiment with tone, composition, and typographic hierarchy to create a cohesive magazine.
