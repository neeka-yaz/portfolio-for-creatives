Borgo Di Milliano

Borgo di Milliano is a local Italian restaurant that brings together the warmth of traditional cuisine and the spirit of community, nourishing both the heart and the stomach. Rooted in family traditions and long-table dining, the Milliano family is devoted to sharing the comfort of home-cooked meals and genuine connection with their neighbourhood.

To extend that feeling beyond the restaurant, Borgo di Milliano introduced a line of their three most beloved sauces—Nonna Marinara, Zio Alfredo, and Sorella Pesto—so guests can bring home a taste of family.

Each sauce is represented through a custom character design that embodies its unique flavour and family role. This approach reinforces Borgo di Milliano’s values of inclusivity and warmth while avoiding depictions of real people.
