Sunshine Coast Lavender

Sunshine Coast Lavender is a small, grassroots body care brand from Sunshine Coast, known for its handmade products and loyal local following. As the company expands into retail, this rebrand focuses on refining its identity and packaging.

The packaging lineup for three cosmetic products was redesigned using sustainable materials such as glass bottles and silk screen printing with soy-based inks. Each box is constructed to protect the glass bottles while acting as the main storytelling surface, highlighting product benefits, ingredients, and the brand’s mission.
